Bio

Mike was born in Toronto, Ontario in 1970. He studied earth science and spent 12 years doing geological and environmental fieldwork in remote areas of Northern Canada. He is passionate about natural history, origins of life, and science in general. Exploring various creative mediums from a young age, he has written music, has produced a cd of guitar compositions, has written a children's book, and has designed and built an architecturally unique off-grid cabin. His sculptural work has been exhibited in galleries in Vancouver, Seattle and L.A., and is part of private collections in Canada and the US. He currently lives in Victoria BC where he is devoted full time to making art.
